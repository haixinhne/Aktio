Nguyễn Thị Duyên

National University of Civil Engineering

We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of solutions for large companies, with a particular on Japanese super general contractors. Our extensive knowledge of the entire process, from planning to sales, design, quotation, procurement, production, and facility management, enables us to deliver tailored services that meet the specific needs of our clients.

Our services are designed to streamline workflows, reduce costs, and improve operational efficiency. We have expertise in constructing easy-to-modify BIM models that facilitate seamless information sharing across teams, as well as visualizing information linked with BIM models. By utilizing cross-departmental efficiencies such as process automation, we can deliver truly valuable output to our customers.
